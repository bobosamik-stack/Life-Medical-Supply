Medical Supply services

Профессиональный вид для сайта: что вы принимаете, проверяете и оформляете.

🧾

Insurance Verification

Проверка insurance plan, eligibility, адреса, телефона и Member ID перед обработкой заказа.

📄

Prescription Intake

Workflow для рецептов от врача: received, missing, expired, needs update.

🩺

Progress Note Tracking

Отметки по medical necessity documentation / progress note, чтобы не терять документы.

📦

Monthly Supplies

Briefs, pull-ups, liners, underpads, gloves, wipes and related medical supplies.

🚶

Mobility & Safety

Canes, walkers, shower chairs, transfer benches, raised toilet seats and bathroom safety.

🔔

Follow-up Reminders

Напоминания: call patient, need Rx, need progress note, insurance update, monthly order due.

Documents required

Prescription, progress note / medical necessity, insurance card, Member ID, current phone and address.
